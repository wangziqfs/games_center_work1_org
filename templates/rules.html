Blackjack Rules:

  • The game is played with a standard deck of 52 cards.
  • Each player receives two cards at the beginning of the game.
  • The dealer only reveals one of their cards initially.
  • You can choose to take more cards (hit) or stay with your current cards (stand).
  • If you go over 21 points, you immediately lose (bust).
  • The dealer must hit until they reach at least 17 points.
  • If the dealer busts, you win.
  • If neither player busts, the one with the highest total wins.
{% elif cur_game=="blackjack_eu" %}

Blackjack_eu Rules:

  • The game is played with a standard deck of 52 cards.
  • Each player receives two cards at the beginning of the game.
  • The dealer receives only one card initially (no hole card).
  • You can choose to take more cards (hit) or stay with your current cards (stand).
  • If you go over 21 points, you immediately lose (bust).
  • The dealer must hit until they reach at least 17 points.
  • Dealer checks for blackjack only after you have finished your hand.
  • If neither busts, the one with the highest total wins.
